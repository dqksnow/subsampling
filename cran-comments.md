This update expands the rare-feature GLM subsampling method in `ssp.glm.rF()`.

sampling criteria, automatic rare-feature detection, response balancing options,
and union-based combination of subsamples.

logistic regression, adds broader tests for rare-feature subsampling scenarios,
and revises the package vignettes.
